Kcee's manager beats up Harrysong's crew publicly (photos)

 Posted on April 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  413 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Following Harrysong’s exit from Five Star music about a week ago, a lot has been going on underground between the label and the music star.

We do not know if Kcee and E-Money were aware of this development but Legit.ng Gossip, exclusively gathered that on Thursday, December 1, Kcee’s manager Soso Soberekon with his boys blocked Harrysong’s manager Desmond Ike and another crew member, Bishop and beat them up.

Speaking with Harrysong’s manager, Desmond he disclosed that while they were leaving the birthday party of the president of Nigerian Football Federation (NFF), Amaju Pinnick which was held at Parkview estate, Ikoyi, Lagos, Soso followed him outside while he was trying to go drive the car and started assaulting him. He added that, Soso accused him of making Harrysong quit the record label but before he could say anything, Soso and his boys pounced on him and the other crew member. At that point, Harrysong who was waiting for Desmond to bring the car came towards the scene but it took the intervention of some comedians to stop Soso and his boys from assaulting Harrysong and his boys. Desmond also revealed that, ever since Harrysong left the label he has been receiving death threats from Soso.

The former label mate and CEO of Alter Plate music, Harrysong confirmed the reported adding that: “I do not know why Soso had to go to this extent but I am sure that it is just a personal beef between him and my manager. Kcee and E-Money would never send him to do such a thing but I am still shocked why Soso went that far.”

However, news also got to us that the Nigerian police has visited Soso Soberekon in his home on Friday, December 2, but he is already on the run but when he was on his phone he promised make himself available at the police station on Monday, December 5, as he is out of town.

Also Soso Soberekon was not the only at Amaju Pinnick's birthday but came along with Kcee and E-Money, as he decided to carry out his devilish act while his 'ogas' were inside enjoying the party.

It could be recalled that earlier in the year, Five Star disclosed that Soso Soberekon has been sacked after he passed across a false information about Skiibii's death. Just wondering why he is still with them tho!
