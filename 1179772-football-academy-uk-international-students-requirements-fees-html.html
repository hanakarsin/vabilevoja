3. Pepsi Football Academy

Pepsi Football Academy provides an excellent opportunity with the annual Pepsi Academy UK Scholarship. It’s a life-changing initiative that may contain your child funds to study in one of the most prestigious football schools in the United Kingdom.

In 2015 and 2016, the scholarship funded the full two years stay at Brooke House College. The Pepsi United Kingdom Scholarship has been a source of motivation for many young minds. It’s their opportunity to get football education in Europe. By the way, Mikel Obi also participated in Pepsi Football Academy and now look at his net worth!

2. Manchester International Football Academy

This institution comes with quite pricey football academy fee. Nevertheless, it’s one of the best places for your child to be raised. What can your child get by entering the academy?

  • He will play football at least 5 days a week
  • Your child will improve his technical abilities
  • Your kid will learn advanced gameplay and tactics
  • Your child will increase his fitness level

The duration of the programs for this particular school may vary from 36 weeks up to two years. What are this football academy requirements?

The entry criteria for all players is the age from 16 to 24 years old. At the same time, your kid should show excellent football skills. If you need to get your child to this football paradise, then you need to contact Jedipel Recruitment Agency.

This agency takes the responsibility of dealing with West African football players. They usually seek for the best players to enter the Manchester International Football Academy.
