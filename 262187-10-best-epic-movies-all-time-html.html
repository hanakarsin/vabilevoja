1. 2001: A Space Odyssey by Stanley Kubrick ,1968

The first entry on our list of best epic movies is Stanley Kubrick’s 2001: A Space Odyssey. 2001: A Space Odyssey presents a strong case as being one of the best movies ever to have graced our movie cinemas. 2001 is a story of the dawn of man. Through the movie, Stanley presents the picture of man as being foolish until the entry of the Monolith. Until then us men could not notice the abundance opportunity of food we had, but soon after the occurrence of monolith we learned not only how to hunt and kill animals for food but also guarantee our survival through them.

Like with all good movies, 2001 has a character that stands out that everybody whose watched the movie always falls in love with; HAL. HAL, the film’s ship computer, is more than just the movie’s villain; he is a sacred being who at times is tempted by our evil being to question not only himself but those who are around him too. If you have not watched 2001: A Space Odyssey there is quite a lot you are missing out when it comes to cool movies. However it is not yet late, visit your cinema today and catch it.

2. Ben-Hur by William Wyler, 1959

Ben-Hur is one of the best epic movies to watch if you haven’t watched it yet. This movie tells the tale of a Jewish man who sought revenge after suffering from a terrible betrayal. It took this exciting Hollywood movie five weeks of filming, and you will need close to four hours to get quality entertainment from it.

3. War and Peace by Sergei Bondarchuk, 1966

The title of the movie “War and Peace” tells you that this is one of the best movies you should prepare yourself to watch. The eight hours long movie is definitely worth every second of your time. Sergey Bondarchuk, the director of the movie, carefully brings out a love story witnessed during the 1812 Napoleon’s invasion of Russia. To confirm its epic nature, the movie has won awards including the Academy Award for Best Foreign Picture during The Cold War period.

4. The Lord of the Rings Trilogy by Peter Jackson, 2001-2003

Among our list of movies, The Lord of the Rings trilogy stands out as one of the best movies ever to be directed in our generation. Peter Jackson has proved to be the best in the epic movie industry by creating an amazingly fictional world that appears real to the audience than their own real world. The Lord of the Rings trilogy storyline is centered on ordinary individuals putting their friendship at risk by fighting for their own greater good. Common themes that the movie focuses on are the power of hope and bonds of friendship. Regardless of your movie genre, The Lord of the Rings series is one of the epic movies that you will never regret spending your time and money on any day!
