The procedure of connecting a Bamba decoder

It is essential to know how to connect the decoder to the TV since it’s the first step of unlocking all your favorite channels on Bamba TV decoder. The first thing you do is point your aerial to the transmission around you. Then connect your antenna to the cable safely securing it to the decoder. Connect the decoder to your TV using the supplied RCA cable.

The power cable provided is the one to connect to the power outlet. You can now set your TV input to AV and enjoy watching Bamba TV channels. It is advisable to re-scan your Bamba TV decoder once in a while to discover the new channels added.

Searching channels on Bamba decoder is quite easy. All you have to do is you go to 'settings', choose 'channel search' and click 'auto search'. If you realize that your picture quality is poor, try seeking the services of a profession for installation of your decoder.
