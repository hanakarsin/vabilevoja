Kano Blast: Victim Accuses 'Strange' White Man

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  134 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

A victim of the Kano Central Mosque attack, Malam Shehu Ahmed, has told Channels Television that a strange white man detonated remotely, the bomb that killed over 100 people at the Kano Central Mosque last week Friday.

Mallam Shehu Ahmed, who is currently receiving treatment at the National Orthopaedic Hospital Dala, Kano, was inside the Mosque when the explosion occurred.

He told the TV station“I saw an uncomfortable white man enter the mosque alongside two black teenage boys who later remotely detonated the explosives in the bodies of the said boys” .

It will be recalled that multiple bombs went off at the Central Mosque in Kano at about 2pm, as Muslims were having their Friday prayers on November 28, 2014.
