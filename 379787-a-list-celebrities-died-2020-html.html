Some see their death coming, others are just caught by surprise. Even in anticipated deaths, it is hard saying goodbye to any loved one, let alone celebrities who have been inspiring and touching thousands of lives as role models. The full list of celebrities who died in 2020 will surprise you with the numbers of high profile deaths you may have missed.

2020 will go down on record as one that we lost influential figures in a row. Even worse, a wave of current affairs lessened the deserved outpouring of tributes to most of these icons. Therefore, it is important to remember them all because their memories will hardly depart from the hearts of supporters.

Celebrities who died in 2020

The world will never remain the same without celebrities that kept societal mantle together for the wellbeing of all. Losing these figures not only destabilizes society but also takes away their irreplaceable talents. Most of these fallen but not forgotten figures come from music, television, sports, and film industries.
