The mother of four initially filed for divorce from celebrity husband Kanye West and the two are reportedly living under the same roof.

The news about the intruder was shared on different publications and according to police officers who spoke to TMZ, the man who tried making his way into the fashion icon's house claimed that he was going to see his wife, Kim.

The incident is said to have happened on Wednesday, February 24, and the culprit only managed to pass through the gate but was intercepted by authority and he never made it to the celebrity's house.

It is yet unknown if the man had a serious crush on the mother of four or he had other ill motives that led to him trespassing.
