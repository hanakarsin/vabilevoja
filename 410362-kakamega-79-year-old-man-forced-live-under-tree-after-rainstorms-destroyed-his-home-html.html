A 79-year-old man in Kakamega has narrated how he has been living under a tree for three weeks after rainstorms destroyed his home.

Alphonse Shivachi said he has been sleeping in the cold for three weeks and has only been getting food occasionally from well-wishers.

Speaking to TV47, the elderly man said his family abandoned him and expressed fear he could continue being homeless for quite some time if no one comes to his rescue.

"The rains ruined my house. For three weeks now, I have been sleeping out in the cold and I only get to eat when someone donates food to me. I have been feeling cold and I am being rained on every single day," the man said.

He pleaded with well-wishers to come to his aid so that he could live a better life.

In a separate report, TUKO.co.ke reported the story of 80-year-old Veronica Wambere and 63-year-old Beatrice Wawira, who lived in abject squalor since their husband Joseph Nyaga died in 2003.

This was made worse because Wawira's children grew up and moved out to live on their own, leaving the family home abandoned.
