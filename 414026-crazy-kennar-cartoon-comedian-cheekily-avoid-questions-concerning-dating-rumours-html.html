Where there is smoke, there is fire, a famous adage goes. Curious netizens thought they spotted the smoke when famous comedians Crazy Kennar and Cartoon Comedian wore matching outfits to a recent event.

The duo’s matching black outfits worn on May 26, 2021, sparked speculation that they are dating.

The fact that couples usually wear matching outfits during their traditional wedding ceremony led netizens to interpret it as proof that the two comedians are an item.

Well, a reporter got to ask the duo the question on the red carpet, and their response was nothing short of cheeky.

Gibberish

Cartoon Comedian was the first to be asked the question, but she deflected it to Kennar.

“Are you guys dating?” the reporter asked Cartoon.“Are we guys dating?” Cartoon instead asked Kennar. “Are we really dating?” Kennar responded before he began speaking gibberish.

The reporter asked the question again, and they both responded in gibberish.

Before the duo responded to the question, MC Jessy had interrupted their interview, playfully saying he had warned them not to.
