Felix Odiwuor, alias Jalang'o wears many hats, including an MC, radio host and comedian.

His exploits have earned him a legion of fans, and he has even been an MC at a function attended by Barack Obama, the former US president.

On June 6, 2021, the accomplished entertainer and his wife Amina Chao stunned netizens after stepping out in matching outfits.

In a photo posted by Jalang'o, the beaming couple was seen side by side at an undisclosed location.

Falling for Amina

The Kiss 100 presenter previously revealed that he fell in love with Amina partly because she treats everyone equally.

“The respect she has towards people who have little to add to her life is the best thing. I loved the way she related to the security guy and the waiters,” he said.

According to Jalang’o, after moving in together, Amina called for a meeting instead of a date. In the forum, she stipulated that they put down their 10-year plan.

Children

Unknown to many people, Jalang’o and Amina have children together.

“Everybody has been telling me to get my wife pregnant. We closed that chapter a long time ago, only that I don’t post my children on social media," he said in January 2021.
