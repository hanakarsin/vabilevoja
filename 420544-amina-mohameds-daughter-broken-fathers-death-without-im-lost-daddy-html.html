The daughter of Sports Cabinet Secretary (CS) Amina Mohamed has penned an emotional eulogy to her late father Khalid Ahmed, who passed away earlier in the day.

The bubbly and fun-loving Firyal Nur took to her Instagram page to express her great loss and to mourn a man she described as a sun and a moon to her.

“You are my sun, my moon, my universe, my beginning, my end. Without you I am lost, daddy,” she wrote.

As previously reported by TUKO.co.ke, Ahmed breathed his last at The Karen Hospital in Nairobi on the morning of Thursday, July 29.

The family confirmed the Ahmed had been receiving at the hospital, where doctors pronounced him dead following the short illness.

In his condolence message to Amina's family, President Uhuru Kenyatta mourned Ahmed as a great person, a gentleman and a strong pillar of his family.

"I have received the disheartening news of the passing away of Khalid Ahmed with great sadness. Ahmed was a great person, a gentleman and a strong pillar of his family. My deepest condolences to my colleague and friend Cabinet Secretary Amina," he eulogised.

Approximately four months ago, the former ambassador and Foreign Affairs Cabinet secretary mourned the loss of her brother Esmael Mohamed Jibril.

Jibril breathed his last on Monday, March 1.
