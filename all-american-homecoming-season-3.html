'All American: Homecoming' Is Renewed for Season 3, but How Many Episodes?

 Posted on July 10, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  662 words  |  Lourie Helzer

When will 'All American: Homecoming' Season 3 air? Season 2 has officially come to a close and The CW renewed the show for a third season.

Spoiler alert: This article contains spoilers for the Season 2 finale of All American: Homecoming.

And just like that, All American: Homecoming Season 2 has officially ended.

It has been quite the journey for Bringston University student Simone Hicks (Geffri Maya). The tennis star first struggled to find her footing on her team, dealt with a breakup with her high school sweetheart, and found herself at odds with her teammates.

Now, Simone has transformed into a well-rounded student, received Thea’s (Camille Hyde) blessing to lead the team, and has even earned her letters as a sister in the sorority Phi Kappa Zeta.

Viewers are excited to see the next chapter in Simone’s collegiate life unfold.

So, when will All American: Homecoming Season 3 air?

Here’s everything that we know.

The CW renewed ‘All American: Homecoming’ for Season 3.

Good news, All American: Homecoming fans!

Although Season 2 has officially come to a close, The CW has decided to pick the show up for Season 3.

“We are thrilled to bring All American: Homecoming back to The CW,” Brad Schwartz, the network’s President of Entertainment said in a statement, per TV Line. Schwartz pointed out that the series is one of the network's "strongest performers across our linear and digital platforms," which is likely why it decided to renew it for a third season.

So, when will Season 3 of All American: Homecoming release?

Interestingly, Season 1 of the OG spinoff was announced on May 24, 2021, and the first episode aired on Feb. 21, 2022. The season concluded on May 23, 2022.

Season 2 was announced on May 12, 2022, and premiered on Oct. 10, 2022. The season officially ended on March 27, 2023.

Given the aforementioned timeline, it wouldn't surprise us if the network releases Season 3 sometime in 2024 (or at least we hope it will). While we don't know when Season 3 will release just yet, we do it will contain 13 episodes.

Following the news of the renewed season, fans of the show took to Reddit to share their excitement. One person wrote, "this is amazing news," while another chimed in, "Yess!!! They couldn’t leave us on that cliff hanger!"

Speaking of cliffhanger, keep reading if you want to find out what happens at the end of Season 2.

‘All American: Homecoming’ Season 2 left fans with a cliffhanger.

Fans of the series have been arguing for weeks on social media about which man is better suited for Simone.

And as viewers have been divided between Damon (Peyton Alex Smith) and Lando (Martin Bobb-Semple), everyone hoped that the season finale would put the debate to rest.

Unfortunately, the season finale has caused even more confusion since the episode ended with a major cliffhanger.

Not only did Simone and Damon finally have a full intimate encounter, but the episode also ended with Simone in the middle of making her choice. Plus, the storyline suggests that JR (Sylvester Powell) may be getting sick again due to a nosebleed, and Coach Marcus (Cory Hardrict) has a wife.

The writing team has a lot to build on for Season 3. Hopefully, we’ll get an answer soon as to who Simone ends up with — Lando or Damon.

All American: Homecoming Season 2 is available to stream on Netflix now.
