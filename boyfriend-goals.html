Hilariously Sweet Boyfriends You Who Are Killing the Relationship Game

10 Prime Examples of Guys Who Are Total Boyfriend Goals

By Robin Zlotnick

Updated Sept. 17 2019, 2:19 p.m. ET

If you found a good dude and he's funny and cute and kind, congratulations! You won the relationship lottery! The people in this list definitely did. Their dudes buy their partners supplies when they need them, bring them snacks when they're hungry, and express their love in appropriately excessive ways. 

So thoughtful

This woman's boyfriend bought her a bunch of supplies she uses, including makeup wipes, to keep at his place for when she comes to stay over. How adorable is that? He even bought her an extra pair of underwear in case she, a grown woman, poops her pants one day. That is love.

Selfie response

When this woman sent her boyfriend a selfie, he had the correct response. He lost consciousness because of how beautiful she is. If your dude falls to the floor because he can't handle your hotness, you know you've found yourself a good one.

A good nurse

If you are sick and you have your period, that's a double whammy. Women know how rough this is. Men should at least buy you several packs of chocolate. The rest of this stuff is above and beyond. As my mother would say, "KEEP HIM!"

Bedtime ribs

Truly, this is the sexiest story I have ever read in my entire life. There is nothing hotter than a man cooking ribs for his partner and then bringing them to her in bed. It's the definition of romance. If I was woken up by my partner at 3 a.m., I would be so mad... until he handed me a delicious, slow-cooked rib. 

Shower pics

I love this so much. Some dudes hear that their partner is in the shower and just want naked pictures, but this guy is dedicated to comedy. And everyone knows that the funniest thing in the world is putting your sudsy hair up in a giant spike. The other funniest thing, if you have long hair, is to fold your hair over your forehead to look like one of the founding fathers. 

Sacrificial pizza

Wow. This is next-level. Because as much as I love my fiancÃ©, the man I am going to marry and the man I love with all my heart, I would never, ever, ever sacrifice my pizza just to tell him how much I love him. Especially if I was drunk. Pizza is precious. 

Urgent burrito

Listen. He knows what's important. She may have been driving, but he was pulling up to Chipotle at that very moment and he needed her order. If he came home without a burrito for her, there's no doubt she would have been mad. He did what he had to do to make sure his wife got the burrito she wanted. 

This is one of the most soothing videos I have ever watched. It's one of those things that I will save and play on repeat when I'm feeling sad. I personally feel cooking for and feeding your partner are the most genuine expressions of love there are. It truly makes me emotional to see.

Chicken nugget dispenser

Speaking of feeding your partner, this guy at the salon is sitting there feeding his girlfriend chicken nuggets while she gets her nails done. That is love right there. She needed her chicken nuggets, and she's incapacitated at the moment. What are boyfriends if not an extra set of hands to perform simple, vital functions for you when you're getting a manicure?

Homemade vanity

This girl's boyfriend kept asking her why she did her makeup on the floor, and when he realized that she didn't have a mirror at chair level, he built her a vanity with a tabletop inlaid with pictures of the two of them. He made it as a surprise when she was away for a week. It just warms my heart.
