Davido Back In Arms Of Ex-Fiance Months After Her Split From King Carter

 Posted on April 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  271 words  |  Hettie Henneman

Nigerian singer, David Adeleke, popularly called Davido and his ex-fiancee, Chioma Avril Rowland, seem to have reconciled their differences and the ‘assurance ship’ is set to sail again.

Naija News reports that this is coming a few months after Chioma’s split from her alleged lover, King Cater.

Over the weekend, Chioma broke the net when she stepped out in style for Davido’s concert rocking his new 30BG pendant worth millions.

The pendant had the inscription, ‘002’, which hinted at her reconciliation with Davido.

A photo that surfaced online on Sunday night captured the mother of one and Davido holding hands together.

This might be good news for many of their fans who are still looking forward to the ‘Assurance Wedding’.

Recall that the singer had earlier proposed to Chioma but the engagement later went south after news broke out that he fathered a fourth child with an American model and make-up artiste, Larissa London.

Sheggz Speaks On Relationship With Bella

Meanwhile, evicted Big Brother Naija ‘Level Up’ housemate, Sheggz, has spoken about his relationship with his love interest, Bella.

The duo were an item in the Biggie House, however many people have berated Sheggz for always abusing Bella emotionally.

Some viewers of the reality show also claimed that their relationship might be a strategy in the game.

During an interview on stage after his eviction on Sunday, Ebuka asked Sheggz about what the future holds for him and Bella, with whom he had a romantic affair in the house.

Responding, the reality TV star, said he does not need to prove anything to anyone adding that Bella remains his ‘babe’.
