Kyndra Sanchez - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on May 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  89 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Film and television actress who is widely recognized for her performance as Yoli Greenberg in the 2021 film Finding Ohana. She also starred on the series Butterbean's Café.

Before Fame

She made her professional acting debut in 2016's Dora and Friends: Into the City!

Trivia

In 2020, she was credited as the Methuen Orphanage Girl on an episode of the critically acclaimed The Queen's Gambit. She was also cast in season 2 of The Baby-Sitters Club.

Family Life

She was born in The Bronx, New York.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Kea Peahu in the film Finding Ohana.
