Louise Marwood - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on March 25, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  102 words  |  Enda Jantzen

British actress best known for her portrayal of Chrissie White (formerly Sugden) on the British soap opera Emmerdale. 

Before Fame

She previously acted under the name Louise Howells and was most notable for her portrayal of Ally Harrison in Hollyoaks: Let Loose. She also appeared in Law & Order: UK, The Bill, Doctors and other British shows while acting under the surname of Howells.  

Trivia

She was previously featured on Emmerdale in the role of Fiona Heath in 2010 while performing as Louise Howells.

Family Life

She grew up in Bristol, England.

Associated With

She played the television wife of Ryan Hawley on Emmerdale.
