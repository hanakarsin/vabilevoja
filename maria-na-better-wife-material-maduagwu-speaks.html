Maria Na Better Wife Material, Maduagwu Speaks

 Posted on July 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  221 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

Nollywood actor, Uche Maduagwu has revealed that Maria of the Big Brother Naija fame is wife worthy.

Maduagwu took to his Instagram account to state that maria is wife material, revealing that most people would praise a successful man but if the table was turned and it was a woman the source of her wealth would be questioned and some might go as far as saying the woman in question is dating their sister’s husband.

He said: “Let us stop this any where belle #face Cuba mumusity #lifestyle for #naija abeg, if a man is successful dem go hail am like obi kunu, but when dem see a woman successful dem go open dem zobo coconut head oversize mouth talk say she is dating dem sister #husband or grandfather wey dey Oba. Maria is hardworking and will never STOOP low to date married men #lekki #bbn6“

This outburst is coming after Cubana Chiefpriest revealed that Maria who is allegedly dating his sister’s husband, kelvin called her to threaten her. The chief priest warned Maria that he won’t take her threat lightly especially if something should happen to his sister.

Most Nigerians have condemned Maria’s actions while others have blamed Cubana Chie priest’s brother-in-law for allowing Maria to go to such lengths.

A video of Maria calling Pere, Kelvin by mistake while in the Bbnaija house has also surfaced online.
