Thomas Brodie-Sangster Married His Wife, Talulah Riley

"Love Is All Around" Thomas Brodie-Sangster and His New Wife, Talulah Riley

Thomas Brodie-Sangster is all grown up! The 34-year-old married Talulah Riley, a fellow actor and Elon Musk’s ex-wife.

By Jamie Lerner

Published June 25 2024, 12:58 p.m. ET

When it comes to heartwarming celebrity news, fans can't get enough of Love Actually and Game of Thrones star Thomas Brodie-Sangster and his recent marriage to actress Talulah Riley. Thomas is known for his timeless roles from when he was a child, and now that he has taken a significant step into a new chapter of his life, fans are eager to know more.

In a world where celebrity relationships often face the spotlight's harsh glare, Thomas and Talulah's love story has been refreshingly genuine. Their journey from dating to tying the knot has captivated hearts, and the excitement surrounding their union is palpable. But this isn’t Talulah’s first high-profile marriage.

Thomas Brodie-Sangster married his wife, Talulah Riley, on June 23, 2024.

Thomas and Talulah exchanged vows in a beautiful ceremony in England per The Sun. Their wedding was a private affair, attended by close friends and family, celebrating their love in an “idyllic village ceremony” at St. George’s Church in Anstey, Hertfordshire. At 38 years old, Talulah is four years older than Thomas — she was 38 on their wedding day, and he was 34.

The duo first sparked dating rumors in 2021 after they met on the set of Disney Plus’s Pistol. They announced their engagement in July 2023 on Instagram, where Thomas wrote, “Love is all around,” a nod to the lyric in Love Actually. They kept details of their relationship relatively private, but have shared the big milestones with the fans.

“We hadn't really acknowledged each other as a romantic possibility or potential until the moment that we both did,” Talulah told The Times. “And it was the same moment a number of months after having worked together and been good friends.” And after Talulah’s tumultuous and unique love life, we bet that Thomas was a refreshing change.

Before marrying Thomas, Talulah was married to Elon Musk twice.

Born in Hertfordshire, England, Talulah's acting career began in the early 2000s. She gained prominence with roles in films like Pride & Prejudice and St. Trinian's. More recently, she played one of Westworld’s scarily human robots.

Talulah’s personal life previously made headlines because of her on-again, off-again marriage to billionaire entrepreneur Elon Musk. Talulah was just 22 years old when she met Elon in 2008 at a posh London club, Whisky Mist. He “wooed” Talulah, but Talulah’s dad did not approve.

“You’ve been picked up by a playboy,” Talulah’s dad said, according to a BBC documentary about Elon. “Text that man that you’re not going for dinner with him and he’s a b-----d.” Even still, Elon divorced his previous wife and the mother of his five children, Justine, and proposed to Talulah. She moved in with him in his Los Angeles mansion and they married in Scotland’s Dornoch Cathedral in 2010. Two years later, however, they divorced.

In 2013, Talulah and Elon remarried in a strange turn of events after they “got back together at the divorce filing.” By 2016, Talulah filed for divorce and they split for good, with Talulah getting a £12.5 million settlement. She then had an on-and-off relationship with 60-year-old artist Matthew Rice before she began a relationship with Thomas.

Congrats to the happy couple!
