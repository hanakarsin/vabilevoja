Done Deals On Deadline Day

 Posted on April 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  469 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

The January transfer window which opened on the 3rd of the month will be ending in the Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga and Ligue 1 today by 11 pm.

The transfer window has seen various football clubs show interest in their preferred players, while some have signed numerous players, others are expected to sign before the market closes today.

Naija News has deemed it fit to share the latest done deals in the January market with you.

Manchester City Sign Julian Alvarez

English Premier League side, Manchester City has announced the signing of Julian Alvarez from River Plate.

However, the Argentine, who celebrates his 22nd birthday today, will remain on loan at Los Millonarios until at least the end of June.

Arsenal Aubameyang Arrives In Barcelona

The former captain for Arsenal, Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang has been spotted in the city of Barcelona ahead of his loan move from Arsenal to Barcelona.

Brentford Sign Eriksen

Brentford on Monday announced the signing of Christian Eriksen on a deal until the end of this season.

Bournemouth Sign Newcastle Goalkeeper, Freddie Woodman

Bournemouth has snapped up Newcastle goalkeeper Freddie Woodman on loan for the rest of the season.

Frank Lampard Confirmed As Everton Manager

Former Chelsea manager, Frank Lampard has been confirmed as their new manager.

The 43-year-old takes over after their former Manager, Rafa Benetiz was relieved of his duty at the Merseyside club.

La Liga Champions, Real Madrid “Agree” To Sign Mbappe

According to Spanish journalist Jorge Calabres, Real Madrid have agreed to sign Kylian Mbappe.

The forward, 23, is expected to join the Spanish giants on a free transfer at the end of the season.

Burnley Sign Weghorst

Burnley have announced the signing of Wolfsburg striker Wout Weghorst.

The 12-cap Netherlands international has agreed to a three-and-a-half-year deal.

Van De Beek Joins Everton

Donny van de Beek has joined Everton from Manchester United for the rest of the season after being denied playing time at Old Trafford.

Kulusevski Signs For Spurs

Dejan Kulusevski has signed for Tottenham Hotspur on loan from Juventus for the rest of the season

Ndombele Joins Lyon On Loan

Ndombele has returned to the side that Spurs originally bought him from. He joins on loan until June with the option to purchase.

Everton Close To Signing Dele Ali

According to football transfer expert, Fabrizio Romano, Alli has agreed to a deal to join Everton on a permanent transfer.

Giovani Joins Villarreal

Giovani Lo Celso has joined La Liga side, Villarreal, on loan for the remainder of the season.

Arsenal Signs Auston Trusty From MLS side, Colorado Rapids

Arsenal have signed Auston Trusty from MLS side, Colorado Rapids. The American signs on a permanent transfer but will stay at the Rapids until May and then will be loaned out in Europe for the 2022-23 season.
