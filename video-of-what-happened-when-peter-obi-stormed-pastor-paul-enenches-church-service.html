Massive Crowd Celebrates Peter Obi At Dunamis Church, Abuja

 Posted on April 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  265 words  |  Hettie Henneman

The Labour Party, (LP) presidential candidate, Peter Obi, on Friday received a shocking reception, at the Dunamis International Gospel Centre vigil in Abuja.

The former Governor of Anambra State has continued to gain popularity amidst the criticisms that his supporters are only on social media.

In the video making rounds online, the entire congregation went agog after the wife of the General Overseer Paul Enenche, Becky introduced Peter Obi.

While addressing members of the church, Becky expressed excitement about having the LP flagbearer in the evening service.

She said: “And we also have tonight, Mr Peter Obi who has joined for the service this evening.”

The entire church erupted into clapping and screaming as they cheered him.

While the congregation were still clapping and cheering Obi, Enenche walked up to him and shook his hand.

2023: Obi’s Victory, A Bigger  Miracle To Nigeria

Peter Obi recently responded to claims by the Peoples Democratic Party, (PDP) presidential candidate, Atiku Abubakar, that only a miracle can make him win the 2023 election.

In an interview on Channels TV, Obi said his political journey has always been characterized by miracles from God.

Obi said he contested the Anambra State Governorship election in 2003 under a party that was barely a year old and he won that year.

The former Anambra governor said he was impeached after six months but he challenged the impeachment in court and also won it when people thought it would be impossible.

He added that his political journey has been a miracle and he’s waiting for the bigger miracle to happen in 2023.
