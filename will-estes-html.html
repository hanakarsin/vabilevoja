Will Estes - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on February 2, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  96 words  |  Lourie Helzer

Actor who played JJ Pryor on the NBC series American Dreams from 2002 to 2005. In 2010, he began starring as Jamie Reagan on CBS's Blue Bloods.

Before Fame

He did commercials as a child, which was his first experience with acting on television.

Trivia

He was nominated for a Young Artist Award several times for his work on The New Lassie and Kirk.

Family Life

He was born an only child to Mary Lu, an office administrator, and Bill Nipper, a projectionist. He and actress Torrey Devitto have dated.

Associated With

He stars alongside Tom Selleck on Blue Bloods.
