YTB Fatt - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on February 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  268 words  |  Hettie Henneman

Rapper who made headlines in 2023 after he was signed to Moneybagg Yo's label BreadGang. He has since earned over 339,000 monthly listeners on Spotify. He released his first major studio album, Who Is Fatt? later that same year on August 8, 2023. The album features songs like, "Had to Do It," "Vanish Mode," "No Lover Boy, and "Poppin It Hard." His single, "Shot Off Gumbo" also earned almost two million plays on the platform within only five months of being released.

Before Fame

He began rapping in the year 2019. At the time, he was struggling with a crippling gambling addiction that set him back thousands of dollars at a time, on occasion. One of the people he used to gamble with turned out to be the cousin of Moneybagg Yo. When the rap mogul arrived at a game himself, they took it upon themselves to play a few tracks for him which, much to his delight, Moneybagg was impressed.

Trivia

The music videos for his songs, "Get Back" and "Shot Off Gumbo" each earned more than 5 million views within four months of being released on his self-titled YouTube channel.

Family Life

On August 5, 2023, he shared a series of photos from his tour on his ytbfatt Instagram account, mentioning in the caption that his mother had joined him for the tour as well. His real name is Cavon Paige. 

Associated With

He collaborated with rapper Rob49 for his track, "Bet I Whip It." In a March 2023 interview with Hot 97, he stated that he is planning to collaborate with rapper Lil Durk in the future as well.
